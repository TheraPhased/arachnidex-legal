ArachniDex Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 29 June 2026

ArachniDex users can request deletion of their account and associated cloud data.

How to request deletion

To request deletion, email:

theraphased@gmail.com

Please include:

  • The email address used for your ArachniDex account
  • A clear request to delete your account and associated data

Example:

I would like to delete my ArachniDex account and associated cloud data. My account email is example@email.com.

What will be deleted

  • Account profile data stored for ArachniDex
  • Display name
  • Avatar image, if uploaded
  • Cloud backup collection data
  • Wishlist and collection data stored in cloud backup
  • Feeding, molt, breeding, sales and notes data stored in cloud backup

What may remain

  • Data stored locally on your own device until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Emails you have sent to support
  • Limited records retained where required for security, legal or operational reasons

We aim to process deletion requests as soon as reasonably possible after verifying account ownership.
