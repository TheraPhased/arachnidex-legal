# Privacy Policy for ArachniDex **Last updated: 12 July 2026** ArachniDex is an app for tracking arachnid and invertebrate collections, including animal records, wishlists, feeding and molt histories, breeding records, value history, milestones, profile information and optional cloud backup. This Privacy Policy explains what information ArachniDex processes, why it is used, how it is stored and the choices available to users. --- ## 1. Information ArachniDex May Process ### Account and profile information ArachniDex may process: - Email address when you create an account or sign in - Firebase authentication identifier - Keeper name or display name - Google profile image, where available through Google sign-in - Custom or premium avatar selection - Account and subscription entitlement status ### Collection information ArachniDex may process information you choose to enter, including: - Animals and species added to your collection - Wishlist records - Custom animal names - Life stage, sex, status and favourite status - Acquisition, vendor, sale and value information - Feeding and molt histories - Breeding records and breeding events - Dated value-history entries - Notes, care information and other details - Custom species records - Animal images or image references associated with records ### Cloud information ArachniDex may process: - Manual cloud backups - Automatic cloud backups for eligible ArachniDex Pro users - Backup timestamps and restore information - Profile information and award progress stored with your account ### Diagnostic and usage information ArachniDex may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand general app usage, diagnose crashes and improve reliability. Depending on the service and device, diagnostic information may include: - App version - Device model - Operating system version - Crash logs and stack traces - Firebase installation identifiers - General app interaction and usage information - Technical information needed to diagnose errors ArachniDex does not receive or store your full payment-card details. Payments and subscriptions are processed by Google Play. ArachniDex may receive information needed to determine whether a Pro purchase or subscription is active, such as the purchased product, entitlement state and transaction status. --- ## 2. How Information Is Used Information may be used to: - Provide collection, wishlist and species-tracking features - Store feeding, molt, breeding and value histories - Provide keeper profiles, avatars, milestones and awards - Allow users to sign in and keep different accounts separate - Check whether a keeper name is already in use - Create and restore manual cloud backups - Create automatic cloud backups for eligible Pro users - Restore account data after sign-in - Verify ArachniDex Pro purchases and subscriptions - Support CSV import and export - Diagnose crashes and technical problems - Improve app stability, usability and features - Respond to support, privacy and account-deletion requests - Protect accounts, backups and app services from misuse --- ## 3. Optional Accounts and Local Use The main collection-tracking features can be used without creating an account. Signing in is optional and is used for features including: - Keeper profiles - Unique keeper names - Cloud backup and restore - Automatic Pro backup - Profile avatar synchronisation - Account-linked awards and profile information Collection data is stored locally on your device unless you choose to use account and cloud features. When you sign out, ArachniDex removes account-specific local collection, profile and award information from the device. Existing cloud data is not deleted simply by signing out. Users are warned to back up unsaved changes before signing out. --- ## 4. Cloud Backup and Restore Signed-in users can manually back up and restore supported ArachniDex data. Eligible ArachniDex Pro users may also receive automatic cloud backups after collection changes. Cloud backups may contain: - Collection records - Wishlist records - Feeding and molt histories - Breeding records - Value-history records - Favourite and status information - Profile and award information - Other supported collection fields Automatic restore may occur after sign-in where the local account database is empty and a valid backup exists. ArachniDex includes safeguards intended to prevent an empty local collection from automatically replacing an existing populated cloud backup. However, users should still keep independent exports of particularly important records. --- ## 5. CSV Import and Export ArachniDex allows users to manually import and export CSV files. Exported CSV files are stored or shared under the user’s control. Users are responsible for protecting exported files and deciding where they are saved or shared. CSV data remains on the device or in a location chosen by the user unless the imported information is later included in a cloud backup. --- ## 6. Firebase and Google Services ArachniDex uses Google and Firebase services, which may include: - Firebase Authentication - Cloud Firestore - Firebase Storage - Firebase Analytics - Firebase Crashlytics - Google Sign-In - Google Play Billing These services process information needed to provide authentication, profile storage, cloud backup, avatar storage, diagnostics, analytics and purchase verification. Google and Firebase may process information on servers located outside your country. Data transfers are handled according to the safeguards and terms provided by those service providers. --- ## 7. Subscriptions and Purchases ArachniDex Pro may be offered through: - Monthly subscriptions - Yearly subscriptions - A one-time Lifetime Pro purchase Monthly and yearly subscriptions are managed and billed through Google Play and may renew automatically unless cancelled through Google Play. Cancelling a subscription does not immediately delete an ArachniDex account or cloud data. Access normally continues until the end of the applicable paid period, subject to Google Play’s subscription rules. Lifetime Pro is a one-time purchase and does not automatically renew. Users can access Google Play subscription management from the ArachniDex Settings screen. ArachniDex does not process or store full card or bank details. --- ## 8. Premium Features and Downgrades If Pro access ends: - Automatic Pro backup stops - Premium selection screens may become locked - Existing supported Pro-created breeding and value-history records remain viewable - Existing data is not intentionally deleted solely because Pro access ended Some editing or creation features may require active Pro access. --- ## 9. Data Sharing ArachniDex does not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers where necessary to operate the app, including Google and Firebase services. Information may also be disclosed where reasonably necessary to: - Comply with legal obligations - Protect users, accounts or app services - Investigate fraud, abuse or security incidents - Respond to valid legal requests --- ## 10. Data Retention Local collection data remains on the device until it is deleted, the app is uninstalled, local account data is cleared or the operating system removes it. Cloud account and backup information is generally retained while the account remains active or until deletion is requested. Diagnostic and analytics information is retained according to the relevant Firebase and Google service settings and retention practices. Some information may be retained for longer where reasonably necessary for: - Security - Fraud prevention - Legal compliance - Resolving payment or account disputes - Enforcing app terms --- ## 11. Security ArachniDex uses Firebase Authentication and account-based security rules intended to restrict account data to the relevant signed-in user. Data transmitted to Firebase services uses secure connections. No electronic system can be guaranteed completely secure. Users should protect their account credentials and keep independent copies of important collection information where appropriate. --- ## 12. After account deletion, some limited information may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, payment disputes, legal obligations or legitimate operational needs. --- ## 13.
