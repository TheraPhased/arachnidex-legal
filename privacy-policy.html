Privacy Policy for ArachniDex

Last updated: 29 June 2026

ArachniDex is an app for tracking arachnid and invertebrate collections, including species records, feeding records, molt records, wishlists, milestones and optional cloud backup.

Information we collect

ArachniDex may collect and store the following information when you choose to use these features:

  • Email address, when you create an account or sign in
  • Display name, if you choose one
  • Profile/avatar image, if you upload one
  • Collection and wishlist records you enter into the app
  • Feeding and molt records you enter into the app
  • Breeding, sales, value, notes and care information you choose to enter
  • Species images or custom animal images you choose to upload or store
  • Cloud backup data, if you choose to use manual cloud backup or restore
  • Firebase authentication identifiers needed to manage your account

How we use information

We use this information to:

  • Provide the app’s collection tracking features
  • Let you sign in and manage your profile
  • Save and restore your collection using cloud backup
  • Show your avatar and milestone award progress
  • Support CSV import and export
  • Respond to support or feedback requests
  • Improve app reliability and functionality

Cloud services and third parties

ArachniDex uses Firebase and Google services for authentication, account management, profile storage, avatar storage and optional cloud backup. These services process data on behalf of the app so that sign-in, backup and restore features can work.

We do not sell your personal data.

Optional data

You can use the main collection tracking features without signing in. Signing in is optional and is used for profile, avatar, backup and restore features.

Avatar uploads, custom images, notes, breeding details, sales details and backup data are user-provided and optional.

CSV import and export

ArachniDex allows you to manually import and export CSV files. CSV files are handled by you on your device unless you choose to back up the data using cloud backup.

Data storage and security

Data stored in Firebase services is transmitted using secure connections. Local app data is stored on your device. Cloud backup data is stored using Firebase services linked to your account.

Account and data deletion

You can request deletion of your ArachniDex account and associated cloud data by contacting:

theraphased@gmail.com

Please include the email address linked to your ArachniDex account. We may need to verify ownership before deleting account data.

When an account deletion request is completed, we will delete account profile data, avatar data where applicable, and cloud backup data linked to that account unless retention is required for security, legal or operational reasons.

Children

ArachniDex is not designed for children under 13.
